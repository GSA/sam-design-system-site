Support page template

The support page template allows developers to layout content instructing users on how to achieve a specific goal or outcome.

Extension of the US Web Design Standards

The US Web Design Standards does not define this page template.

Note: Extends the default page template.

Guide Pages

Videos and Images

Informational based and used with text and images. Give users control of the video. Consider what users should do at the conclusion of the video. Make the video short and to the point. First impression is crucial. Users must understand what they are watching and see its value. Provide context around the video content.2

User Guides

  • Do not make content too big or too small.
  • Provide pictures and diagrams.
  • Make it clean, with an efficient use of white space, and use a sans-serif font.
  • Organize information hierarchically.
  • Use a glossary of technical terms.
  • Define symbols, icons, and codes.
  • Write out sequential steps in an accurate order.3

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) Guidelines

  1. Answers reply with a simple yes, no, sometimes, maybe, and it depends.
  2. Make it convenient to submit new questions and report issues/bugs.
  3. Make sure outdated answers expire and get rid of duplications.
  4. Track the most top issues and the most want or need to know issues and place the most wanted answers to the top of the page.
  5. Add jump links (when necessary) or another form of in-page or site-wide navigation. Create links between FAQs.
  6. When there is a long FAQ page and short answers, use a separate page to list out the questions and group answers by category. Organize by topic if more than one issue per category.
  7. Provide filters and sorting tools.4

Human (Phone and Chat)

Make sure the user gets a quick response time and one trained representative to answer their chat or call. Representatives should answer in a polite tone. Use metrics to monitor conversations. Always improve on the process.

Forums

Implement a search within the Forum. Make a place for users to exchange ideas, help each other troubleshoot, and post issues.
