The .gov means it's official.
Federal government websites always use a .gov or .mil domain. Before sharing sensitive information online, make sure you are on a .gov or .mil site by inspecting your browsers' address(for "location") bar.

This site is also protected by an SSL(Secure Sockets Layer) certificate that's been signed by the U.S. government. The http:// means all transmitted data is encrypted - in other words, any information or browsing history that you provide is transmitted securely.
