<h1>Graphics</h1>
<h2>Photography</h2>
<p>Photography is used on the Home Landing Page and Section-Level Landing Pages. When selecting photographs, choose images that embody the SAM.gov tagline &quot;SAM.gov is the official U.S. government website for people who make, receive, and manage federal awards.&quot;</p>
<p>Photographs should include the following attributes:</p>
<ul>
<li>One or more people of diverse attributes with positive facial expressions</li>
<li>Engaged in activities related to U.S. Government grants or contracts</li>
</ul>
