Styles Buttons

Use buttons to signal actions.

Primary Action

Use major action buttons for the most important buttons the users will look for in order to initiate an activity (e.g., Create Listing) or complete an activity (e.g., Submit). Typically no more than one button in this color should appear on the page, although there may be occasions to modify this rule. Major actions buttons generally appear at the top of a page when the action is to initiate work, or at the bottom of the form, such as when the action is to submit that form.

Secondary Action

They represent useful, but not necessary actions the user may want to take. Generally, these buttons are represented by an icon and/or related term. Examples include actions such as 'Edit', 'Subscribe', 'Favorite', or 'Share'.
